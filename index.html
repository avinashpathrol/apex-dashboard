Understanding
Trading Signals
The bot uses 6 technical indicators. It needs at least 3 signals in the same direction to place a trade.
ℹ️
Scoring: Each indicator votes bullish or bearish. 3+ bullish = BUY. 3+ bearish = SELL. Anything else = HOLD.
RSI — Relative Strength Index
Both
Measures momentum 0–100. Very low = oversold (likely to bounce). Very high = overbought (likely to drop).
BULL → RSI < 35 or RSI < 50 and rising
BEAR → RSI > 65 or RSI > 50 and falling
Window: 14 periods × 15min
MACD — Moving Avg Convergence Divergence
Both
Tracks two moving averages. When MACD crosses above signal line = momentum shifting bullish. Below = bearish.
BULL → MACD crosses above signal OR already above + histogram positive
BEAR → MACD crosses below signal OR already below + histogram negative
Settings: 12 / 26 / 9
EMA-20 — 20-Period Exponential Moving Average
Both
Average price of last 20 candles (5 hours). Price above it = short-term uptrend. Below = downtrend.
BULL → Price > EMA-20
BEAR → Price < EMA-20
EMA-50 — 50-Period Exponential Moving Average
Both
Medium-term trend (12.5 hours). EMA-20 above EMA-50 = golden cross (uptrend). Below = death cross (downtrend).
BULL → EMA-20 > EMA-50 (uptrend)
BEAR → EMA-20 < EMA-50 (downtrend)
Bollinger Bands — Volatility Envelope
Both
Two lines around price based on volatility. Near lower band = statistically stretched down, likely to bounce. Near upper = likely to drop.
BULL → Price in bottom 20% of BB range
BEAR → Price in top 20% of BB range
Settings: 20 period, 2 std deviations
Volume Confirmation
Confirming
Doesn't trigger trades alone. Confirms the dominant direction when volume is unusually high.
Fires when: current volume > 1.3× 10-candle average
BULL → High vol when more bullish signals
BEAR → High vol when more bearish signals
Step by Step
How Each Trade is Taken
Every 7–15 minutes the bot runs through this exact process.
1
Fetch Market Data
Gets last 100 OHLC candles (15-min) for BTC/CAD from Kraken. ~25 hours of history for accurate indicators.
2
Calculate 6 Indicators
RSI, MACD, EMA-20, EMA-50, Bollinger Bands, Volume all calculated fresh every run.
3
Score Signals
Each indicator votes. 3+ bullish AND bullish > bearish → BUY. 3+ bearish AND bearish > bullish → SELL. Else → HOLD.
4
Check Balance
BUY needs ≥ $20 CAD. SELL needs actual BTC. If missing → SKIPPED (intentional protection, not an error).
5
Execute Market Order
Places a $20 CAD market order on Kraken. Fills instantly at the best available price.
6
Send Telegram Alert
Instant message with price, quantity, confidence and P&L. Fires even when dashboard is closed.
7
Log Summary
JSON summary written to GitHub Actions logs. Dashboard reads these to show history and stats.
⚡
Confidence Score
How certain the bot is
confidence = 55 + (dominant / total) × 45

3 of 3 signals = ~85%. 4 bullish + 1 bearish = slightly less. Max capped at 95%. Risk is LOW if 4+ align, MEDIUM if exactly 3.

📉
Worst Case on $20 CAD
If BTC drops −2% before SELL signal:
$20 × −2% = −$0.40 + ~$0.10 fees = ~$0.50 max loss

At $200 CAD that same loss = $5.00 — still very manageable.
📈
Best Case on $20 CAD
If BTC rises +3.5% and bot catches the SELL:
$20 × +3.5% = +$0.70 − $0.10 fees = ~$0.60 net profit

At $200 CAD = +$6.00 per trade. Multiple trades/day compounds quickly.

Scenario 3 — SKIPPED
Signal fires but can't execute
SKIPPED — Balance issue (BUY): Wants to buy but CAD is $0 — already in an open trade. No double buying.

SKIPPED — No BTC (SELL): Wants to sell but BTC balance is 0. Nothing to sell.

Neither is an error — it's the bot being responsible.
Quick Reference
Glossary
Plain-English definitions for every term on the dashboard.
BUY / SELL / HOLD
BUY — Purchased BTC. SELL — Sold all BTC. HOLD — Not enough signals. Waiting.
SKIPPED
Signal fired but couldn't execute. No CAD to buy, or no BTC to sell. Click the badge in History for details.
Unrealised P&L
Profit/loss on your open position if you sold right now. On paper until bot sells.
Realised P&L
Locked-in profit/loss after a SELL. Real money in your account.
Confidence %
How strongly signals agree. 88% = 88% of signal weight points in trade direction.
Take Profit (TP) +3.5%
Reference level on In Trade chart. Bot exits on signals, not this fixed %. Shown at +3.5% above entry.
Stop Loss (SL) -2%
Reference level at −2% below entry. If price hits here, bearish signals usually follow naturally.
Market Order
Executes immediately at the best current price. Used for instant fills.
Win Rate
% of completed trades that were profitable. 60% = 6 of every 10 trades made money.
OHLC
Open, High, Low, Close — the 4 price points per 15-minute candle used for calculations.
